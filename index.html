Find Your New Best Friend

We connect rescued pets with loving homes through weekly adoption events, online listings, and outreach with local partners. Start here to learn why adoption saves lives and how you can help.

Why Adopt?

  • Saves lives and reduces overcrowding in shelters and rescues.
  • Supports responsible ownership and community wellness.
  • Many adoptions include initial vaccines and microchipping.
  • You gain a loyal companion while making room for other animals in need.

New to adoption? Read background info on Animal adoption (Wikipedia) to understand typical processes and best practices.
