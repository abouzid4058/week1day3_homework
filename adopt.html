Adoption Requirements & Resources

Thinking about adopting? Review these essentials and browse the package options. For general background, see Animal adoption (Wikipedia).

What You’ll Need

  1. Valid ID and proof of residence.
  2. Landlord approval if you rent your home.
  3. Agreement to provide routine veterinary care and training.
  4. Transportation crate or safe travel plan for your new pet.

Helpful Tips

  • Bring photos of your living space to discuss suitability.
  • Ask about temperament, energy level, and medical history.
  • Be open to senior or special-needs pets — they’re wonderful companions.
